Launch the "omnipong.exe"
	if you choose "player vs player" you launch a normal game of pong with "A" and "W" to control player one and "Down arrow" and "Up arrow" for moving the pong
	if you choose "player vs AI" you will play the game against an AI on level 1 once you win or lose the AI will switch to level 2 and after that level 3 and, finnaly you will return to the menu
	if you choose "player vs AI" with a level selected you will play with that AI difficulty and the game will not switch to the nect level and not return to the menu if you either win or lose
if you want to go back on the menu press "escape"
if you want to quit the application press "escape" on the menu scene
